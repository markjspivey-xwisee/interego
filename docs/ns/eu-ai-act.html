Terms

eu-ai-act:AccuracyAttestation Class
Attests measured accuracy/robustness/cybersecurity properties of an AI system per Article 15. Issuer is the conformity assessment body.
eu-ai-act:appliesToSystem Object property
Links a regulatory annotation to the AI system descriptor it concerns.
eu-ai-act:Article12 Individual
Citable IRI for EU AI Act Article 12 (automatic, lifetime logging ensuring traceability of functioning). Used as a dct:conformsTo control target in compliance evidence; realized structurally by eu-ai-act:LoggedAction.
eu-ai-act:Article15 Individual
Citable IRI for EU AI Act Article 15 (appropriate accuracy, robustness and cybersecurity over the lifecycle). Used as a dct:conformsTo control target in compliance evidence; realized structurally by eu-ai-act:AccuracyAttestation.
eu-ai-act:Article50Disclosure Class
End-user-facing disclosure: this content was produced by an AI system, this image is synthetic, this chatbot is not a human, etc. Each customer-facing AI interaction descriptor SHOULD carry one.
eu-ai-act:articleReference Datatype property
Free-text article + paragraph reference, e.g. 'Art 14(4)(a)' for granular pinning.
eu-ai-act:assessedAt Datatype property
eu-ai-act:ConformityAssessment Class
The output of a Title III Chapter 5 conformity assessment procedure. Cites all underlying attestations (DataGovernance, Accuracy, RiskManagement) via prov:wasDerivedFrom. The composed certificate descriptor IS the assessment.
eu-ai-act:DataGovernanceAttestation Class
Attests that training/validation/testing datasets meet Article 10 quality criteria (relevance, representativeness, freedom from errors, statistical properties). Issuer typically a data steward.
eu-ai-act:hasHumanReviewer Object property
eu-ai-act:HighRiskSystem Individual
Annex III categories: biometrics, critical infrastructure, education, employment, essential services, law enforcement, migration, justice + democratic processes.
eu-ai-act:HumanOversightCheckpoint Class
A descriptor recording that a human reviewed an AI action prior to consequential effect. Carries the reviewer's identity (AgentFacet), decision (SemioticFacet modal: Asserted=approved, Counterfactual=rejected), and timestamp. Required for high-risk system actions per Article 14.
eu-ai-act:LimitedRiskSystem Individual
eu-ai-act:LoggedAction Class
An action descriptor that satisfies Article 12 record-keeping: includes attributed agent, timestamp, input + output references, model identifier, and a tamper-evident anchor (iep:supersedes chain + ZK or blockchain anchor).
eu-ai-act:MinimalRiskSystem Individual
eu-ai-act:RiskClassification Class
Classifies an AI system's risk tier per Article 6: prohibited / high-risk / limited-risk / minimal-risk. Attaches as a semiotic facet on a iep:ContextDescriptor that describes a system or action. Drives which other Articles' obligations apply.
eu-ai-act:RiskManagementRecord Class
Records the establishment and operation of a continuous, iterative risk management system. Each record cites prior versions via iep:supersedes; the chain IS the audit trail.
eu-ai-act:TransparencyDisclosure Class
Records that the deployer was provided with the instructions, capabilities, limitations, and accuracy expectations of the AI system at deployment time.
